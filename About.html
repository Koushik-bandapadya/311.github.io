Project Description:

Enterprise Resource Planning

Enterprise Resource Planning is a tool that maintains a database of information related to business processes anywhere from human resources to customer relationship management. In general, ERP uses a centralized database for various business processes to reduce manual labor and to simplify existing business workflows.By using an ERP, data from multiple departments can be easily shared and visualized across an organization. This wealth of information and simplification can assist in the development of business goals and reduce the amount of time that your employees spend on tasks that could be automated.There are many features in a modern ERP software. However for our project, we will use the following features:

1. Human Resources: information about the employees, their contacts, salary, hiring date, tax etc

2.Customer relationship management : basically a tool to collect customer data during sales and show them products (by sending email from their sales information) they frequently buy by analysing their buying records depending on the product stock

3. Inventory management : current stock of the products available in the warehouse. This tool will also show how frequently a product needs to be restocked. The stock will be updated real time during sale or during movement from warehouse to another.

4. Sale: basically selling a product to a customer, print out an invoice of makin a sale. During sale, a database will keep information of the customer and feed it to customer relationship management tool for retaining the customer to buy the product again. At the same time, the inventory management tool will also reduce the stock of the products sold.
